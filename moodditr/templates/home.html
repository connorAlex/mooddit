mooddit calculates sentiment by using VADER Sentiment Analysis. VADER utlizies social media datasets to calculate sentiment into a compound score. Scores can range from -1 to 1 (extremely negative to extremely positive). User and subreddit scores have been divided into 4 categories:

  • Extremely Negative / Positive
  • Moderately Negative / Positive
  • Somewhat Negative / Positive
  • Neutral

Each user's entire comment history is calculated for the scoring. Subreddits are scanned by calculating all comments for the top 25 posts of the current month.

Hutto, C.J. & Gilbert, E.E. (2014). VADER: A Parsimonious Rule-based Model for Sentiment Analysis of Social Media Text. Eighth International Conference on Weblogs and Social Media (ICWSM-14). Ann Arbor, MI, June 2014.
